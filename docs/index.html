qn_data

# Load data set
qn_data_desc = readWorksheetFromFile("./data/data_overview.xlsx", sheet = "qn_data", 
    startCol = 1, endCol = 0)

# Create table
kable(qn_data_desc) %>%
    kable_styling(bootstrap_options = c("hover"), font_size = 14, fixed_thead = T)
Variable_Name Variable_Meaning Values
ID Participant Identifier each number represents one participant
age Participant’s age measured in years
sex Participant’s gender 1 = male, 2 = female
mum_ed Educational level of participant’s mum 1 = no degree 2 = secondary modern school [German: “Hauptschule”] 3 = middle school [German: “Realschule”] 4 = advanced technical college entrance qualification [German: “Fachhochschulreife”] 5 = higher education entrance qualification (A-levels) [German: “Abitur”]
dad_ed Educational level of participant’s dad 1 = no degree 2 = secondary modern school [German: “Hauptschule”] 3 = middle school [German: “Realschule”] 4 = advanced technical college entrance qualification [German: “Fachhochschulreife”] 5 = higher education entrance qualification (A-levels) [German: “Abitur”]
income family income 1 = 0-1250€ 2 = 1250-2000€ 3 = 2000-3000€ 4 = 3000-5000€ 5 = > 5000€
occ_mum Current occupation of participant’s mum 1 = student 2 = skilled worker [German:Gelernter “Facharbeiter”] 3 = foremen/forwomen [German: “Vorarbeiter”] 4 = clerk in the middle grade of the civil service [German: “Beamter mittlerer Dienst”] 5 = senior officials [German: “Beamte höherer Dienst”]
occ_dad Current occupation of participant’s dad 1 = student 2 = skilled worker [German:Gelernter “Facharbeiter”] 3 = foremen/forwomen [German: “Vorarbeiter”] 4 = clerk in the middle grade of the civil service [German: “Beamter mittlerer Dienst”] 5 = senior officials [German: “Beamte höherer Dienst”]
wink_ind Winkler index Scoring range: 1-15
PPVT_Perc Peabody Picture Vocabulary Test percentile Percentile range: 1,1 - 98,9
CMM_Perc Columbia Mental Maturity Scale percentile Scoring range: 1-100
WM Working memory score Scoring range:
EMK_EK_P Parental rating emotion knowledge Scoring range: 0-12
EMK_EM_P Parental rating empathy Scoring range: 0-24
EMK_EK_Ch Child assessment emotion knowledge Scoring range: 0-28
EMK_EM_Ch Child assessment empathy Scoring range: 0-19
SRS Social Responsiveness Scale Total Score [raw] Scoring range: 1-95 (until 60: normal functioning)
SCQ Social Communication Questionnaire Total Score [raw] Scoring range: 0-39
random_EEG Participant’s randomization order R1 = randomization 1, R2 = randomization 2
